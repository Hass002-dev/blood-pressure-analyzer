Hear about Hass

Welcome to creation of guy who look at the very nice internet and think, 'You know what this is missing? My big chaos.' His journey in web making no start because he want to change digital world. It start because he find his most best hobby is to build completely broken things from zero, just so he can get the big crazy heart-pumping feeling when he finally fix it!

He always saying that 'simple logic save lot of time', which is very funny because every day he accidentally make the most crazy, no-logic puzzles for himself. Then he have to play Sherlock Holmes to solve his own mess.

He fall in love with coding because it is the most close a normal guy can be to real magic wizard. He type strange magic words inside the glowing black box, and BOOM! He summon a working app from nowhere! Or actually, he just summon invisible gremlin who steal half his buttons, and then he must fight the computer until it listen.

It is very wild rollercoaster. One minute he feel like biggest genius Einstein, and next minute he is total confused potato. And he is 100 percent addict to this. He build this Blood Pressure Analyzer so you can check your health, which is very funny irony because making this app almost destroy his own heart and blood pressure. Please drink water, take big breath, and enjoy app!
