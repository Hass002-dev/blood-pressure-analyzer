Understanding your Blood Pressure readings can save your life from an unexpected death.

Normal

If your Systolic is less than 120 AND Diastolic less than 80.
You are in the optimal range! Keep up the great work with your healthy habits.

Elevated Numbers

If your Systolic is 120 -129 AND Diastolic less than 80.
Your numbers are slightly above normal. This is a great time to focus on a balanced diet and regular exercise to prevent hypertension.

High Blood Pressure (Stage 1)

If your Systolic is 130 – 139 OR Diastolic 80 – 89.
You have hypertension. It is highly recommended to consult with a healthcare professional about lifestyle changes or medications.

Hypertensive Crisis

If your Systolic is 180+ OR Diastolic 120+.
Stop what you are doing and seek emergency medical assistance immediately. Do not wait for your numbers to come down on their own.

Healthy Habits for Better Blood Pressure

  • Reduce Sodium:

    Keep your salt intake low to prevent your body from holding onto excess fluid, which raises blood pressure.

  • Stay Active:

    Just 30 minutes of walking a day can make a massive difference in keeping your heart strong.

  • Manage Stress:

    Deep breathing, meditation, or simply taking a break from staring at broken CSS code can help lower your numbers!

  • Hydrate Regularly:

    Drinking plenty of water helps your heart pump blood more easily through your blood vessels.

  • Consult a Professional:

    This app is a great tracking tool, but always talk to your doctor or a healthcare professional for actual medical advice.
